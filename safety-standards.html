FaceOff 24 Child Safety Standards

FaceOff 24 is committed to protecting children from exploitation, abuse, and harmful content. The following policies and practices are in place to ensure our platform is a safe space for all users.

1. Zero Tolerance for CSAE

We do not permit any content or behavior that sexually exploits or endangers children. This includes:

  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
  • Sexualization of minors
  • Grooming or predatory behavior

2. Reporting & Moderation

All users can report inappropriate content or behavior through the in-app reporting tools. Our moderation team reviews reports promptly and takes action, including removal and account suspension or banning.

3. Proactive Monitoring

We use both automated systems and human moderators to detect and remove content that violates our child safety policies. We also collaborate with relevant law enforcement agencies where necessary.

4. Age Restrictions

FaceOff 24 is intended for users aged 18 and over. We prohibit underage users from creating accounts, and any suspected underage activity is investigated and removed.

5. Contact Information

To report CSAE-related concerns or abuse, please contact us directly at:

Email: support@faceoff24.com
