FaceOff 24 - Child Safety and Abuse Prevention Policy

At FaceOff 24, we are committed to providing a safe and secure environment for all users. We do not tolerate any form of child sexual abuse, exploitation, grooming, or inappropriate behavior.

Our Safety Practices

  • We prohibit users under 18 years of age.
  • We use automated tools and manual moderation to detect and remove harmful content.
  • Users can report abuse directly within the app.
  • All reports are reviewed by our safety team.
